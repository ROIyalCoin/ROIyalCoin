ROCO Core integration/staging repository
=====================================

ROCO is a brand new digital currency that combines all the positive aspects of successful digital currencies, while taking into account their flaws and weak points.
The project brings tangible benefits not only to the digital world but also to the real one.


More information at [roiyalcoin.pro](https://roiyalcoin.pro) Visit our ANN thread at [BitcoinTalk](https://bitcointalk.org/index.php?topic=4517612.0), join our group at [Discord] (https://discord.gg/S9adMgS).
